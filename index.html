Our Mission

"Provide the bright minds with a competitive platform in order to prove their mettle." To provide a veritable forum, Conducting workshops, interactive seminars and conducting Olympiads and non-curriculum study material for all students.

Our Vision

As an Educational Organization our aim is to build a competitive spirit along with a focus on developing healthy academic competition among students. To bring about a steady and speedy change in the very way education is currently imparted in the country.

Our Values

Stimulate healthy competitive spirit with the help of reward based contests and olympiads.Promote student's participation in national/international programmes.Establish a more interactive and practical approach to science and IT in classrooms.
